Shrek's Tribute Page

Shrek's Biography

Name: Shrek

Date of Birth: May 23, 1975

Place of Birth: Swamp of Duloc

Parents: Swamp Creatures (names unknown)

Occupation: Ogre, Swamp Protector, Hero

Height: 7 feet

Weight: 500 pounds

Known For: The Shrek film series

Early Life and Background

Shrek was born on May 23, 1975, in the remote Swamp of Duloc. His parents, both swamp creatures, raised him with a deep appreciation for solitude and nature. From a young age, Shrek learned the importance of self-reliance and developed a love for his swamp home.

Adolescence

During his teenage years, Shrek faced the challenges of being different from the humans and magical creatures that inhabited the nearby villages. Despite the fear and prejudice he encountered, Shrek maintained his independence and embraced his unique identity as an ogre.

The Turning Point: Meeting Donkey

In 2001, Shrek's life took a dramatic turn when he met Donkey, a talkative and loyal companion. This unexpected friendship began when Shrek reluctantly saved Donkey from being captured by Lord Farquaad's soldiers. This encounter set the stage for Shrek's first major adventure.

Rescuing Princess Fiona

Later in 2001, Shrek embarked on a quest to rescue Princess Fiona from a dragon-guarded tower. Initially motivated by the promise of reclaiming his swamp from Lord Farquaad, Shrek's journey led to a deeper connection with Fiona. The two eventually fell in love, and their union defied traditional fairy tale conventions.

Date of Marriage: April 15, 2002

Family Life

Shrek and Fiona settled into their swamp home and soon started a family. Their first child, Fergus, was born on September 9, 2003, followed by twins Farkle and Felicia on January 19, 2005. Shrek embraced his role as a father, balancing his ogre instincts with the responsibilities of parenthood.

Date of Retirement: May 23, 2020 (on his 45th birthday)

Legacy

Shrek's legacy extends beyond his heroic deeds. He taught the world that true beauty lies within and that everyone, no matter how different, deserves love and acceptance. His story continues to inspire new generations, proving that even the unlikeliest of heroes can change the world.
