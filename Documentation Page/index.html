Early Life and Background

Shrek was born on May 23, 1975, in the remote Swamp of Duloc.

His parents, both swamp creatures, raised him with a deep appreciation for solitude and nature.

From a young age, Shrek learned the importance of self-reliance and developed a love for his swamp home.

The Turning Point

In 2001, Shrek's life took a dramatic turn when he met Donkey, a talkative and loyal companion.

This unexpected friendship began when Shrek reluctantly saved Donkey from being captured by Lord Farquaad's soldiers.

This encounter set the stage for Shrek's first major adventure.

Rescuing Princess Fiona

Later in 2001, Shrek embarked on a quest to rescue Princess Fiona from a dragon-guarded tower.

Initially motivated by the promise of reclaiming his swamp from Lord Farquaad, Shrek's journey led to a deeper connection with Fiona.

The two eventually fell in love, and their union defied traditional fairy tale conventions.

Date of Marriage: April 15, 2002
Family Life

Shrek and Fiona settled into their swamp home and soon started a family.

Their first child, Fergus, was born on September 9, 2003, followed by twins Farkle and Felicia on January 19, 2005.

Shrek embraced his role as a father, balancing his ogre instincts with the responsibilities of parenthood.
